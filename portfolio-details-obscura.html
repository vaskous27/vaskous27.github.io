Musical Instruments for Jerks

Project Info

Obscure Musical Instruments E-Commerce Site. After my obsession with the Theremin and one of our team's exceptional Accordion skills, it was decided the world needed an online store, which would sell obscure instruments from all over the world. The site features categories from all ends of the planet with instruments like Didjeridus and Wheelharps.

  • Built-On: PHP, CodeIgniter and MySQL.
  • Tasks: Developed Concept, Project Manager, Created Database, Coded back-end and front-end.
