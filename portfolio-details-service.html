Service Ninja

The Burning Man of Businesses

Project Info

Business bartering for services. The Craigslist meets Burning Man of businesses. In this project we wanted to give startups an advantage by offering them a platform where they can exchange services for services instead of monetary exchange. After extensive research, we discovered there are no good platforms out there, which offer this, with only a few mostly allowing the exchange of just products and therefore found a demand in the market. A lot of new companies struggle with raising capital and therefore this would be a good alternative for getting the services they need to lift off the ground. It's all about helping each other grow.

  • Built On: MEAN stack, MongoDB
  • Tasks: Developed Concept, Created database, Project Manager, Designed Parts of front and back-end.
